Pricing

  • Short Film

    • Flat Rate:
      • Low Budget (under $10,000 total budget): $500–$1,000
      • Medium Budget ($10,000–$50,000 total budget): $1,000–$3,000
      • High Budget (over $50,000 total budget): $3,000–$5,000
    • Per Minute of Music:
      • $100–$300 per minute
      • Average length: 10–15 minutes of music
    • Hourly Rate:
      • $25–$50/hour for smaller projects or clients preferring hourly billing

  • Feature Film (Flat Rate)

    • Low Budget Feature (Under $100,000 total budget): $2,000–$5,000
    • Medium Budget Feature ($100,000–$500,000 total budget): $5,000–$15,000
    • High Budget Feature (Over $500,000 total budget): $15,000–$40,000+

    Note: The flat rate works well when the total film budget is known, and it’s easier to estimate the amount of work based on the project scope.

  • Feature Film (Per Minute of Music)

    • Low Budget Feature: $200–$400 per minute
    • Medium Budget Feature: $400–$700 per minute
    • High Budget Feature: $700–$1,200 per minute

    Note: More complex or cinematic scores will be at the higher end. A typical feature requires 30–60 minutes of music, or more, depending on the film's length and genre.

  • Commercial

    • 1-Minute Commercial: $300–$1,500
      • Low Budget: $300–$800 (small business or online ads)
      • Medium Budget: $800–$1,200 (regional brands or small TV ads)
      • High Budget: $1,200–$1,500+ (national brands, high production values)

    Note: High-end commercial rates can go even higher depending on production scale. Expect a short but impactful score with quick turnaround times.

  • Video Games

    • Small Indie Game (2–10 minutes of music): $100–$500 per minute
    • Mid-Size Indie or Mobile Game (10–20 minutes of music): $500–$1,500 per minute
    • Large or AAA Game (20+ minutes of music): $1,500–$5,000 per minute

    Note: Prices can vary significantly depending on project scope and complexity. Some indie games may offer royalties or licensing deals instead of flat fees based on the game's potential success.
